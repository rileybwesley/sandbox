About Papa Bear Power

Built on trust, powered by experience. We're your neighbors — and your electricians.

Our Story

Papa Bear Power started with a simple idea: do great electrical work, be honest about it, and treat every home like it's your own. What began as one electrician and a truck has grown into a trusted name in our community.

We chose the name "Papa Bear" because that's exactly how we approach every job — with protective instincts, strength, and a deep commitment to keeping families safe. Your home's wiring isn't just a job to us. It's the system that keeps your family comfortable, connected, and protected.

We're not a big national chain. We're your neighbors. We live here, work here, and raise our families here. When you call Papa Bear Power, you're getting a team that genuinely cares about doing the job right.
